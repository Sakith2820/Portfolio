JavaFX Chowbay GUI





“Chowbay” is an online self-food ordering system which aims to replace the traditional takeaway systems at hotels and restaurants so customers can easily pay for and buy their favorite meals. This system records every detail of the order in an efficient and user – friendly manner. We considered the convenience of both customers and suppliers so this will be useful for both parties. This project has considerable scope and can be modified and updated with future demands. ​

  • Characteristics of the project
  1. User – friendly
  2. Fast and Secured
  3. Lots of food varieties for customers
  4. 24/7 service
  5. Can use both money and credit/debit cards
  6. Less paperwork


  1. Problem Solved

    2. According to the information we gathered from our research which we did as a group we saw that people don’t like to wait in ques for a long time to pay for the meals. Also, they don’t like to go to the cafes or restaurants just to order a simple coffee or juice because it’s both time and money consuming. We have also noticed that sometimes shops are closed when customers wants meals, especially at night and early in the morning.

    So, considering all the above problems we have concluded that with the knowledge of Java, to build an application to provide 24/7 food service including bakery items, coffee and other drinks. We hope to expand this application to a level that we can deliver a full meal to customers doorstep. Also, we are considering merging more cafes, hotels and restaurants with our application so that the customers can have more choices. ​

  2. Technologies Used

  • Back – end: My SQL
